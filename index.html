About the Master Guide

This is one of the most current leadership development program for youth ministry in the Seventh-day Adventist Church. The Master Guide is the foundation for youth ministry leadership, then builds two levels of continuing education that will help keep youth leaders sharp.

Basic requirements to become a Master Guide

  • 1. Must be a baptized member, in regular standing, of the Seventh-day Adventist Church.
  • 2. Must be above or at least 18 years old on or before February 1, 2025.
  • 3. Must be active in church or any religious programs.

SY:2024-2025 Activities

Investiture Program

MMA Pathfinders, and the Master Guide Club of Mindanao Mission Academy hold its Investiture Program last May 4, 2024. The program serves as the culmination to recognize all of their sacrifices during the training. For the Master Guide Trainees, the said ceremony will allow them to take one step higher, from being a Pathfinder to become a full member of the world-wide organization of the Master Guide Club whose main purpose is to carry on the "Salvation" & "Service" to the community.
